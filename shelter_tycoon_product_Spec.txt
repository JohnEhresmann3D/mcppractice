Product Specification: ShelterVille
Executive Summary
ShelterVille is a free-to-play, web-based farming simulation game inspired by Farmville, reimagined as a virtual animal shelter management experience. Players build, expand, and manage their own shelter, rescuing real adoptable animals via APIs, caring for virtual pets, and fostering adoptions—both in-game and in real life. The game promotes animal welfare awareness, integrates live adoption data, and drives donations/revenue to partner organizations like Paws Your Game (70% of net proceeds) and sponsored shelters (30%), with your operational cut covered via efficient ad models.
Key Differentiators:

Real-time API integration for adoptable pets (e.g., RescueGroups.org).
Hybrid virtual/real impact: In-game "adoptions" link to shelter donation prompts.
Social virality like Farmville (visiting friends' shelters, gifting supplies).
Proven feasibility: Builds on HTML5 tutorials and Phaser.js open-source farming games.

Launch Goal: MVP in 2-3 months, targeting 100K users Year 1 via X/TikTok virality and Paws Your Game partnerships. Projected revenue: $50K+ from ads/donations.
Game Overview
Players start with a small rundown shelter in a customizable world. Rescue animals from real shelters, feed/groom them with "crops" (pet food/supplies), build enclosures, and match pets to adopters. Daily logins yield rewards, with social features encouraging visits and collaborations. Emotional hooks: Heartwarming stories, progress from "overcrowded" to "no-kill haven."
Tagline: "Build Your Shelter, Save Real Lives."
Platforms: Web browser (HTML5), progressive web app (PWA) for mobile/desktop. No downloads needed.
Genre: Casual simulation (farming + tycoon + social).

Core Gameplay Loop

Login/Check-In: Claim daily rewards (food/supplies).
Rescue: Browse API-listed pets, "rescue" to shelter (costs energy/coins).
Care: Plant/harvest "supply crops," feed/groom pets (timed growth like Farmville).
Build/Expand: Use earnings to upgrade enclosures, add play areas.
Adopt Out: Match happy pets to virtual adopters; prompt real donations.
Social: Visit friends, gift items, co-op rescues.
Progress: Level up shelter, unlock rare breeds/events.

Energy System: Regens over time; refill via ads/light activity.

Updated Art Direction & Visual Style
Core Aesthetic: Modern Cartoon Network-inspired 2D cartoonism—bold, clean, and energetic. Draw from:

Thick black outlines with minimal or no shading (flat colors for that punchy, readable look).
Vibrant, oversaturated palette: Neon greens for grass, candy-pink kennels, electric blues for skies, and pops of yellow/orange for happy accents.
Exaggerated proportions: Animals with huge sparkling eyes, floppy ears that stretch comically, and squash/stretch on actions (e.g., a dog leaping with limbs flailing like rubber).
Backgrounds: Stylized and whimsical—think hand-painted but simplified, with dynamic elements (bouncing clouds, wobbly fences) to keep it lively.
Influences: Adventure Time's quirky charm + We Baby Bears' adorable fluff + Gumball's mixed-media playfulness (occasional photo-real pet photos from APIs blended in cartoon frames for humor).
